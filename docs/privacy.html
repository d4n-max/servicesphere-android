Information You Enter

ServiceSphere may store information that you choose to enter into the app, including:

  • Business profile details
  • Client contact details
  • Job details and notes
  • Quote and invoice data
  • Photos added as job proof
  • Client signatures
  • Reminder settings and scheduled reminder data
  • Generated PDF files
  • Exported JSON or CSV files

Local Storage

ServiceSphere is designed to store core business records locally on your device. The app does not currently provide cloud sync and does not automatically upload job, client, quote, invoice, photo, or signature records to a ServiceSphere cloud service.

Generated PDFs and data export files are created locally on your device. If you choose to share those files or messages through Android's share sheet, the selected receiving app or service may process that information according to its own terms and privacy policy.

Subscriptions

If subscriptions are enabled, purchase and subscription status may be handled by Google Play and RevenueCat. ServiceSphere may use subscription status to unlock Pro features. Google Play and RevenueCat may process purchase-related data according to their own policies.

Data Sharing

ServiceSphere does not sell personal data. Core local business records are not automatically shared. Data is shared only when you choose to export, send, or share content from the app, or when subscription services are used for purchase verification.

Export and Deletion

ServiceSphere includes in-app tools to export local data and to delete local records from the device. Deleting local data removes app records from local storage, but it may not remove files or messages you previously shared with other apps, services, clients, or backups outside ServiceSphere.

Security

Because ServiceSphere stores records locally, you are responsible for protecting access to your device, backups, exported files, and shared PDFs/messages. Use your device lock screen and avoid sharing exports with untrusted recipients.

Changes

This policy may be updated before or after release. The current version should be hosted at the privacy policy URL used in Google Play Console.
