## Full Description
ServiceSphere helps solo trades and small service crews keep daily field work organized from one Android phone.

Manage clients, schedule jobs, create quotes, send invoices, capture photo proof, collect client signatures, and keep local records together without needing a desktop system. ServiceSphere is designed for service businesses that want a practical field service app, job management tool, quote maker, estimate maker, invoice maker, and work order app in one focused workspace.

Create client records with contact details and notes. Turn incoming work into jobs, track status, schedule visits, and use calendar reminders to see what is coming next. Build professional quotes with line items, tax, discounts, and totals, then convert accepted quotes into invoices when the work is ready to bill.

ServiceSphere also helps you document the job. Add photo proof from the camera or gallery, capture client approval with signatures, and generate clean PDF quotes and invoices that can be shared through Android's share sheet. Use follow-up message templates for quotes, invoices, completed jobs, and review requests so client communication stays consistent.

Your core business records are offline-first and stored locally on your device. You can export local data for backup or delete local records from inside the app when needed.

Core features:
- Client management for service-business contacts
- Job scheduling and job status tracking
- Quote and estimate builder with line items
- Invoice builder with tax, discounts, and totals
- Professional quote and invoice PDF generation
- Share quotes, invoices, and follow-up messages
- Photo proof for completed or in-progress work
- Client signature capture for approval
- Calendar view and job reminders
- Local data export and delete tools
- Offline-first local records

ServiceSphere is suitable for handyman services, contractors, cleaners, mobile repair, installers, maintenance providers, and other small field-service businesses that need simple client management, contractor invoice tools, handyman invoice workflows, job scheduling, photo proof, and signature capture from a phone.

ServiceSphere does not currently include payment processing, cloud sync, team dispatch, accounting integrations, tax/legal/accounting compliance tools, or route optimization.
