# Google Play Feature Graphic Spec

## Requirements
- Size: 1024 x 500 px
- No tiny text
- Keep important content centered
- Use ServiceSphere purple `#7C3AED` and indigo `#4338CA` branding
- Show phone mockup or clean document/job cards
- Avoid clutter and excessive UI detail
- Export as PNG or high-quality JPEG

Suggested headline: "Jobs, Quotes & Invoices — From Your Phone"

Suggested subline: "Built for solo trades and small service crews"

## Design Direction
Use a clean SaaS/business style. Show a phone mockup with simplified Dashboard, Jobs, Quote, or Invoice cards. Add subtle supporting cards for photo proof, signature approval, and PDF sharing. Keep contrast high and composition centered for Play Store cropping.

## Generation Prompt
"Create a premium Google Play feature graphic for ServiceSphere, an Android field-service app for solo trades and small crews. Use purple #7C3AED and indigo #4338CA branding, clean SaaS style, phone mockup showing dashboard/jobs/invoice cards, and headline: Jobs, Quotes & Invoices — From Your Phone. Professional, trustworthy, modern, not cluttered."
